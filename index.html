How does POP.VIP Saving Work?

When you save your POPG Tokens in POP.VIP, you earn an annual rebate based on a
percentage of tokens you commit. These rebates in POPG Tokens are released daily,
contributing to a cap that you can redeem your chosen entertainment services with.

* Penalties for early saving termination apply.

Example 1

Peter buys 60,000 POPG Tokens and saves 50,000 for 12 months in POP.VIP.

Peter qualifies for the Gold VIP Tier for 1 year.

  • Peter can claim up to 5,000 POPG Tokens (50,000 * 10%) in rebate during his savings term of 12 months.
  • He can receive 100% rebate of his favourite entertainment services, up to 416.667 POPG Tokens every month, for 12 months.
  • Peter will also receive 600,000 VIP Fan Points (VFPs) (50,000 * 12) which can be used to participate in the upcoming airdrops and claim more POPG Tokens.

Example 2

Jenny buys 20,000 POPG Tokens and saves all of it for 6 months in POP.VIP.
Jenny qualifies for the Ruby VIP Tier for 6 months.
  • Jenny can claim up to 500 POPG Tokens (20,000 * 5% * 0.5 yr) in rebate during her savings term of 6 months.
  • She can receive 100% rebate of her favourite entertainment services, up to 83.333 POPG Tokens every month, for 6 months.
  • Jenny will also receive 120,000 VIP Fan Points (VFPs) (20,000 * 6) which can be used to participate in the upcoming airdrops and claim more POPG Tokens.
